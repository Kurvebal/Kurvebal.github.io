Splinter

Wise beyond his years, Splinter is a pacifist zen dog who now resides in Philadelphia. Born in unknown circumstances in California, Splinter was rescued in Philadelphia by his owner, Josh Rosen. He enjoys hiding under the bed, going to doggy daycare at Ruff Life Philadelphia, and alerting Josh to danger outside (followed by hiding under the bed)

Splinter is shy at first, but eventually warms up to be a most welcome influence on all of those around him.
